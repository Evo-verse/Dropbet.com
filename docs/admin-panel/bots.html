14. Bots

Purpose

The bot system allows automated gameplay on the platform, simulating real player activity based on pre-set configurations. Bots can help create constant engagement and maintain active game sessions without manual input.

Creating a New Bot

  1. Go to the Bots section in the admin panel.
  2. Click Add Bot.
  3. Fill in the required fields:
    • Account Name – Select or enter the name of an existing player account that will act as a bot.
    • Status – Choose Active or Inactive.
    • Bot Type – Defines average session duration and bet size:
      • Minnow – ~2 hours, minimum bets
      • Dolphin – ~4 hours, medium bets
      • Whale – ~6 hours, high bets
    • Behavior Pattern – Determines how the bot places bets and changes games:
      • Casual – Small changes in bet size, switches slot after losing streaks.
      • Regular/Normal – Steady betting, rarely changes slots unless a big win or long play occurs.
      • Chaser – Increases bets after losses to recover.
      • Highroller – Keeps bets within a fixed high range until hitting big.
      • Trickster – Randomly changes between all patterns.
    • Session Start Time – The UTC time when the bot begins playing.
    • Active Days of the Week – Select which days the bot will be active.
    • Games – Choose from enabled slot games.
  4. Click Confirm to save the bot.

Editing an Existing Bot

  1. In the Bots list, click on a bot to open its settings.
  2. Modify fields such as Status, Bot Type, Behavior Pattern, schedule, or games list.
  3. Click Save to apply changes.

Bot Behavior Rules

  • All bot bets run through the internal aggregator, bypassing the external slot aggregator (no external commission fees).
  • Only enabled slot games can be selected.
  • Start times are randomized daily within ±1 hour of the configured time.
  • Bots can take random breaks up to 1 hour during a session.
  • Decision-making during play follows the selected behavioral pattern:
    • Evaluate game state.
    • Decide to increase bet, decrease bet, or switch slot.
