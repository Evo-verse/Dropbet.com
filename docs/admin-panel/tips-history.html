9. Tips History

The Tips History section allows admins to view all tipping transactions between users and admins on the platform. It is divided into two tabs: Users and Admins.

Users Tab

Displays tips sent between users.

Displayed Information:

  • Sent by – Username and avatar of the sender.
  • Received by – Username and avatar of the recipient.
  • Amount – Tip amount.
  • Currency – The currency used for the tip (e.g., DBC).
  • Public – Shows whether the tip was public (Yes) or private (No).
  • Created At – Date and time when the tip was sent.
  • More Info – Clicking the three dots opens detailed transaction info, including transaction ID, payment provider status, and any related metadata.

Admins Tab

Displays tips sent or received by admins (same format as Users tab), allowing tracking of any admin participation in tipping.
