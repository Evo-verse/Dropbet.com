6. Streamers

The Streamers section is dedicated to managing and tracking users who are flagged as “is streamer” in their profile. This view allows admins to closely monitor the performance and earnings of partnered streamers who bring traffic to the platform.

Period Statistics

The top section provides key performance metrics for the selected period, similar to the Affiliates dashboard but specific to streamer accounts:

  • Referrals GGR / Wager / Bet Count / Bet Average – shows how much revenue and betting activity streamers’ referrals have generated.
  • Affiliate Referrals Deposits Count / Amount / Average – deposit activity from the streamer’s referred players.
  • Affiliate Referrals FTD Count / Amount / Average – first-time deposits linked to the streamer’s referral code.
  • Affiliate Admin Tips – manual tips or bonuses granted by admins.
  • Affiliate Referrals Count / Active Referrals Count – total and currently active referred users.
  • Affiliate Reward Taken – rewards claimed by the streamer.
  • Affiliate Total Withdrawals – total withdrawn earnings.

These stats help assess each streamer’s direct contribution to platform growth.

Streamer List Table

The lower table lists all streamers with:

  • Users – streamer username, profile picture, ID, and VIP level.
  • Active Referrals – number of referred users currently active.
  • Total Referrals – total number of referred players.
  • Total Deposits Under Code – sum of deposits made under the streamer’s referral code.
  • Total Withdraws / Total Fills – withdrawals and deposits made by the streamer themselves.
  • Total LTV of Player – lifetime value of the streamer as a player.
  • Profit/Loss – net profit or loss generated by the streamer’s activity.
  • Wager / GGR – total bets placed and gross gaming revenue generated.
  • More Info – opens detailed streamer account data.
