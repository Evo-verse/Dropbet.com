13. FAQ Management

The FAQ Management section allows admins to create, edit, and organize Frequently Asked Questions displayed to users.

Main Features:

  • Add FAQ – Opens a form to create a new FAQ entry with a title and text.
  • Sort – Drag-and-drop to reorder FAQs. The order determines how they are displayed to users.
  • Title – The FAQ question shown to users.
  • Text – The detailed answer to the FAQ question. Supports long-form explanations.
  • Actions – Edit or delete an FAQ entry.
  • More info – Displays metadata or additional configuration options for the FAQ.

Usage Example:

  1. Click Add FAQ.
  2. Enter the Title (question).
  3. Enter the Text (answer) with clear and concise information.
  4. Click Save to publish it.
  5. Drag entries to reorder them if needed.
