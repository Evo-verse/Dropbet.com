VIP Program – Submissions Page

Overview

This page lists all players who have submitted the VIP Program application form. It serves as an overview for admins to review and access detailed player information.

Layout & Columns

  • Username – The registered username of the applicant on DropBet.
  • Site player – The casino or platform where the player is currently active (can be external, e.g., Stake, Stake.us, etc.).
  • Total wagered (lifetime) – Total amount the player has wagered historically on their current site.
  • Total wagered (recent period) – Amount wagered in a defined recent timeframe (e.g., last month or during a promotion).
  • Actions (⋮) – Click to view the full submission details.

Search & Filtering

  • Search by username – Enter the player’s username to quickly locate a specific application.
  • Search button – Executes the search query.

Actions Menu

For each row, the ⋮ menu opens the VIP Submission Details view, showing the complete form responses provided by the player.

VIP Submission Details View

The details page is structured into four main sections based on the original form:

Step 1 – Stay in touch method

  • Applicant’s real name.
  • Preferred communication method (e.g., Email, Telegram).
  • Contact details / username on the stated platform.

Step 2 – Site information

  • Current site(s) the player uses.
  • Amount wagered in the last month.
  • Reasons for leaving or staying with their current site.

Step 3 – Play style

  • Average deposit size in USD.
  • Weekly frequency of play.
  • Preferred game types (slots, house games, live games, etc.).

Step 4 – Statistics information

  • VIP progress screenshot from their current site.
  • May include level, percentage progress, total games played, number of wins, etc.
