Deposit Bonus

Overview

The Deposit Bonus page allows administrators to monitor and manage all player deposit bonus activities. This includes viewing who has claimed bonuses, tracking wagering progress, checking expiration dates, and updating bonus statuses when needed.

Page Layout & Features

1. Deposit Bonus List

The table displays all users who have been assigned or claimed a deposit bonus, with the following columns:

  • Username – Player’s account name or email (with avatar).
  • Deposit Amount – Qualifying deposit amount made to activate the bonus (if applicable).
  • Bonus Amount – Value of the bonus credited to the user.
  • Bonus Claim Date – Date the player claimed or received the bonus.
  • Bonus Active Until – Expiration date for the bonus; once reached, the bonus is no longer usable.
  • Wagered – Amount already wagered toward the bonus requirement.
  • Wager Required – Total wagering amount needed before the bonus can be fully unlocked for withdrawal.
  • Status – Current bonus state (ACTIVE, CLAIMED, CANCELLED).
  • Actions – Three-dot menu for administrative actions (e.g., cancel bonus).

2. Statuses & Their Meanings

  • ACTIVE – Bonus is valid and wagering requirements are being tracked.
  • CLAIMED – Bonus credited but not actively in play; may require user action to activate.
  • CANCELLED – Bonus voided by admin or due to terms violation; no longer valid.
